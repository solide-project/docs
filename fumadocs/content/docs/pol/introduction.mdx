## Overview

Proof of Learn (PoL) is an interactive Web3 education platform that offers hands-on learning through real-world tasks like deploying smart contracts and completing on-chain transactions. Progress is verified on-chain, and learners earn PoL POAP NFTs as proof of achievement. Built on Open Campus, PoL promotes transparency, collaboration, and open-source innovation, making it the go-to platform for mastering Web3 across multiple ecosystems.
