## Overview

StylIDE is a browser-based IDE for Stylus smart contract development. It enables writing, compiling, and deploying Stylus contracts with ease, offering a seamless experience for developers. With integrated support for Stylus Testnet and MetaMask deployment, StylIDE simplifies the development process while ensuring smooth interaction with the Stylus blockchain.
