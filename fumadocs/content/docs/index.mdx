## Overview

Proof of Learn (PoL) is a blockchain interactive learning platform that offers hands-on education across 100+ blockchains, featuring an multichain environment for immerse learning experiences. Learners earn POL POAPs NFT as on-chain proof of achievement, ensuring verifiable and rewarding education. Powered by Open Campus.
